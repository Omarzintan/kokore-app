About LanguageSpace

Our Mission

LanguageSpace is dedicated to the preservation, documentation, and revitalization of the Dagaare language, a language spoken primarily in Ghana and Burkina Faso.

What We Do

Dictionary

Comprehensive Dagaare-English and English-Dagaare dictionary with phonetic spellings and example sentences.

Translation

Accurate translations with contextual examples to help learners understand proper usage.

Community

Building a community of speakers, learners, and researchers interested in preserving the Dagaare language.

About Dagaare

Dagaare is a Gur language spoken by approximately 1-2 million people in the Upper West Region of Ghana and southwestern Burkina Faso. It belongs to the Niger-Congo language family and features a rich tonal system and complex grammatical structures.

Despite being spoken by a significant population, Dagaare, like many African languages, faces challenges in the digital age, including limited online resources, standardization issues, and decreasing usage among younger generations.
